Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 Oct 2025
Controller: Lunvi Labs (“we”, “us”, “our”)

Lunvi Labs provides server-side event delivery (Meta Conversions API, GA4 Measurement Protocol, etc.). We focus on privacy-first tracking: regional hosting, consent support, and minimized identifiers.

1. What data we collect

We collect two categories of data:

  • Lead / contact data you submit: When you request an upgrade, demo, or contact us, you may send us: company name, work email, phone number, billing / tax details, and free-text notes. We use this to respond, create an account/offer, and handle billing.
  • Technical / analytics data: We measure basic product interest on lunvilabs.com — e.g. which plan was requested, which form was submitted, and approximate page usage. We currently use Google Analytics 4 with IP anonymization and consent mode. We do not try to personally identify you from this analytics data.

2. How we use this data

  • To contact you about access, pricing, onboarding, or support.
  • To understand demand (which plans / regions are requested).
  • To maintain security and reliability of the service.

3. Legal basis (GDPR / KVKK style)

We rely on legitimate interest to understand product demand and keep the service secure, and contract / pre-contract obligation to contact you about onboarding and payment. When local law requires consent for certain measurement cookies, we respect Consent Mode and downgrade signals to non-identifying pings until consent is granted.

4. Data location & retention

We aim to host customer event data in-region (for example EU region) when requested. Contact / lead submissions are stored securely in our CRM / inbox so we can follow up. We keep lead data for as long as it's relevant for sales or legal record-keeping, then we delete or archive it.

5. Sharing & subprocessors

We may use vetted infrastructure providers (cloud hosting, email, analytics). Where required, we sign Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) and limit access to what is necessary to operate the service. We do not sell personal data.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction, deletion, or restriction of that data
  • Export of that data
  • Objection to certain processing (for example, marketing follow-ups)

To exercise these rights, email us at info@lunvilabs.com.
