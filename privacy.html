Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 Oct 2025
Controller: Lunvi Labs (“we”, “us”, “our”)

Lunvi Labs provides server-side event delivery (Meta Conversions API, GA4 Measurement Protocol v2, Event Router, etc.). Our platform focuses on privacy-first tracking: regional hosting, consent enforcement, and minimized identifiers. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have.

1. What data we collect

We collect two main categories of data:

Lead / contact data you submit. When you request an upgrade, start a demo, or contact us, you may send us:

  • Company name
  • Work email / phone number
  • Billing / tax details and invoicing info
  • Free-text notes (questions, integration requirements, etc.)

We use this to respond, create an account/offer, provision infrastructure, and handle billing / support.

Technical / analytics data. We measure basic product interest on lunvilabs.com — e.g. which pricing plan was viewed, which form was submitted, approximate page usage, and aggregated performance metrics for our endpoints. We currently use Google Analytics 4 with IP anonymization and Consent Mode v2 style behavior. We do not attempt to personally identify you from this analytics data.

2. How we use this data

  • To provide the service. Set up server-to-server tracking containers, custom domains (CNAME), SSL, event routing and debugging for you or your organization.
  • To reply and support. Answer questions, share onboarding docs, schedule technical reviews, and deliver SLAs where applicable.
  • To maintain security & compliance. Detect abuse, audit delivery, respect consent signals, and keep regional data residency (e.g. EU) where required.
  • To invoice. Generate or update quotes, pro/enterprise agreements, and billing records.

3. Cookies, consent, measurement

We aim to send conversion / analytics events using consent-aware, server-side calls instead of browser trackers wherever possible. In practice:

  • We honor Consent Mode–style “granted / denied” signals and fall back to anonymized pings if consent is denied.
  • We anonymize IPs for GA4 and avoid storing raw ad identifiers unless you explicitly provide them for matching (for example, hashed email or event_id dedup keys).
  • We may still use strictly necessary cookies or similar tech for security, session continuity, anti-fraud, and load balancing.

4. Data residency & retention

  • Regional hosting. Many customers request EU-region routing and storage. We can isolate a container / endpoint in specific regions (for example EU) to meet data residency requirements.
  • Retention. Operational logs, delivery traces, and audit trails are kept only as long as needed for debugging, billing, compliance, and SLA enforcement — then periodically minimized or deleted.
  • Backup / disaster recovery. We maintain secure backups for continuity, which may briefly retain data after primary deletion.

5. Who we share data with

  • Service providers / subprocessors. We rely on infrastructure (cloud hosting, logging/alerting, email delivery, analytics) to run Lunvi Labs. We require confidentiality and security commitments from these providers.
  • Compliance / legal. We may disclose minimal necessary information if required by law, regulation, court order, or to defend our rights.
  • No selling of personal data. We do not sell your contact details as a lead list.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you’re located (for example under GDPR / KVKK), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct or update inaccurate information
  • Request deletion (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Request a copy (portability)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

We’ll respond to requests in line with applicable law. For security, we may need to verify that you’re the rightful contact for the account / company.
