FAQ

How does dedup work?▾
We match browser identifiers (event_id / fbp / fbc) with server-side events to avoid double counting. Our templates are compatible with sGTM and GA4 MP v2.
What’s covered in your Consent Mode v2 support?▾
We automatically respect consent signals and use anonymous pings when denied, upgrading to full measurement once consent is granted.
How do you manage custom domains (CNAME) and SSL?▾
Bring your domain, we provision & auto-renew certificates and handle regional routing.
What if I exceed my plan?▾
We’ll notify you and keep events flowing. You can upgrade or add event blocks anytime.
