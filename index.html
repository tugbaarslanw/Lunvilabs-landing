Frequently asked questions

Do I need to host sGTM myself?▾
No — we provide managed sGTM hosting with EU-region option. You can also bring your own.
Do you support Consent Mode v2?▾
Yes. We normalize events to consent status. When consent is denied, we still send anonymous pings that can be upgraded later.
Can I send to Snapchat / TikTok?▾
Yes — TikTok Events API, Snapchat CAPI, Klaviyo and raw webhooks are all supported.
What if I exceed my plan?▾
We’ll notify you and keep events flowing. You can upgrade or add event blocks anytime.
